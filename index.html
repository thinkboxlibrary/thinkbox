Vidya / Parent
My son has been a member of ThinkBox Library for almost 14 years. ThinkBox Library played a huge role in inculcating the habit of reading in him. The impact of this library on him is very evident. Vasumathi V. / Headmistress, Shemrock Happy Hearts play school
When I was trying to inculcate reading habits to the preschoolers of age two years to 6 years in my preschool four years back, I found it a herculean task to find the perfect genre of books that will create the interest for the little ones to start their reading journey. However, help came to me in the form of Mrs Bhakti Shah, the owner of ThinkBox Library. She provided me with well-catalogued books for each age group. Her expertise and passion for running a children's library for the past 13 years, helped us to create more urge to read books to the tiny tots. We also conducted field trips to her library, which our kids used to love and wait so that they could touch, feel, read and get lost in the world of children's books waiting for them in Thinkbox.
